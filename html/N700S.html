Shinkansen N700S

The Shinkansen N700S, also known as the "Supreme," is a high-speed train developed by the Central Japan Railway Company. It offers advanced safety, speed, and comfort features, including a maximum operating speed of 300 km/h. The N700S introduces improved energy efficiency, noise reduction, and passenger-friendly amenities, making it one of the most innovative trains in the Shinkansen series.

Why Choose the Shinkansen N700S?

The Shinkansen N700S provides a revolutionary experience in high-speed rail travel. Designed to reduce air resistance and energy consumption, it offers quieter and smoother rides, even at high speeds. Its active suspension technology enhances stability, and its design accommodates rapid evacuation in emergencies. Additionally, the N700S provides modern conveniences like power outlets at every seat, adjustable lighting, and spacious seating. Choosing the N700S means experiencing one of the most comfortable and efficient high-speed trains in the world.
